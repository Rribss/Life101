  • Upcoming Features

This is where you can take a look at what we have planned for the future. All functions listed below are coming soon!

  • A subscription service will soon be added for a more personalized experience including one-on-one dicussions, and even paid contributions to our website(payment can be discussed)
  • Once we store up enough articles, a filter system will be added to look for a specific range of articles that you are looking for!
  • There will be a discussion webpage where you can make an account and talk with those who have similar circumstances as yourself.
  • As a little add-on to the above statment, our article database shall be ever-changing, as we live off of our consumers, who look to get their problems solved. If their problem hasnt been solved by what we already have, they can submit their own situation through the submission form on the homepage under the introduction paragraph!
  • Later on, We'll be making a system of podcasts for those who would like to share their experiences having mental disorders or any issues they've had in their lives. They can even express their own thoughts and solutions as well!
  • While we have only talked about mental health, we will gradually branch out to other areas of adulthood like physical health(going hand in hand with mental health), finance, real estate, education, politics, security, and even careers! Like we said, this website is meant for everyone to be prepared to be the best adult they could be so they have no regrets when the wrinkles come.
